Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil (optional but you better use that shit)
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups dried malfada noodles
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:

  1. Step 1:

    Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5-7 minutes. Drain and discard grease. Add spaghetti sauce, tomatoes, onion, garlic, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Cook over low heat until sauce is hot, about 15 minutes.

  2. Step 2:

    Meanwhile, fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Cook mafalda noodles at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain.

  3. Step 3:

    Add cooked and drained noodles to the sauce and stir until completely coated. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.

  4. Step 4:

    Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.

  5. Step 5:

    Place skillet under the hot broil and cook until cheese is golden and bubbly, 3-5 minutes.
